What We Do

Modernizing Hotel Event Collaboration

Every module is designed for the specific workflows of hotel sales, conference services, catering, and event planners.

Room Block Transparency
Track pickup in real time, set automated alerts at 70% pickup and at 14/7-day cutoff milestones. See every unreserved room at risk.
Structured Event Detailing
Manage menus, setup approvals, and streamlined BEO revisions — from initial draft through final client sign-off in one place.
Interactive F&B Planning
Dynamic menu selection, auto-calculate live estimates with service charge and tax, and send for approval in a single step.
Client Portal Experience
A premium, branded event dashboard for planners — countdown, deadlines, approvals, and messaging. No more email chains.
Document Management
Contracts, BEOs, amendments, and invoices — all in one place with version history, e-sign flows, and role-based access controls.
Event-Scoped Messaging
In-platform threads keep all hotel-client communication inside the event workspace. With read receipts, attachments, and meeting requests.
